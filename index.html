The History Of Our Hotel

The Marriott company started as a root beer stand opened by J.W. and Alice S. Marriott in 1927. The first hotel was the Twin Bridges Motor Hotel, which opened in Arlington, Virginia, in 1957. Today, there are more than 3,000 hotels worldwide.
