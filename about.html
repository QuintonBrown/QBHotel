About QB Hotel

RECEPTION OPENING HOURS

We do not have a standard reception with a clerk available at all times. There is, however, a receptionist at the hotel during our opening hours who may be easily contacted. Our rooms should be ready for check-in no later than 15.00. Check-in outside the opening hours of the reception should be arranged in advance - Please let us know your arrival time in the comments field when placing your booking, and we will send you the check-in details with your booking confirmation.

MEAL DEAL

We have no restaurants in the hotel, but we have made some great deals with some of Bergen's best restaurants for you to choose from. In addition, we have breakfast arrangements with nearby cafes. See the info channel on the TV in your room, or pick up a brochure in the reception.

CLEANING AS REQUIRED

Naturally, when you arrive at the hotel, your room will be clean; the bed made with freshly laundered cotton bed linen, and there will be one bath towel per guest. Daily cleaning services are not included in the room price, but during longer stays there is a weekly cleaning service. Additional cleaning services and a change of bed linen/towels may be ordered at an extra fee.

BREAKFAST IS NOT INCLUDED

Breakfast is not included in the room price, and we do not serve breakfast at our hotel. You may purchase a coupon for breakfast at a nearby bakery for 110,- NOK during the opening hours of the hotel reception, or order one when you make your room reservation.

There is an electric kettle in every room (remember to bring your own coffee/tea). There is also a vending machine in the reception where you can purchase refreshing drinks and snacks.
